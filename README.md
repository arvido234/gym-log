# Gym Log 

**Gym Log** is a modern, minimalist Android fitness tracker designed to simplify your workout routine. Built with a focus on speed and usability, it helps you organize exercises by day, log sets quickly, and visualize your progress without unnecessary clutter.

---

##  Features

* **Weekly Scheduler:** Organize your split effortlessly. Assign exercises to specific days (Mon-Sun) and sort them to match your workout flow.
* **Smart Logging:** Quickly log Sets, Reps, Weight, and RPE. Customizable input fields allow you to hide metrics you don't use.
* **Visual Progress:** Track your strength gains over time with interactive **History Charts** for every exercise.
* **Premium Dark Mode:** A "Deep Charcoal" & "Electric Blue" theme optimized for gym environments â€” easy on the eyes and battery-efficient.
* **Privacy First:** 100% Local. No accounts, no cloud dependency, no ads. Your data stays on your device.
* **Localization:** Fully localized in **English** and **German**.

---

##  Tech Stack

* **Language:** Java (Android SDK)
* **Data Persistence:** Room Database (SQLite)
* **UI Components:** Material Design 3 (Material You)
* **Charts:** MPAndroidChart
* **Architecture:** MVVM (Recommended for Room)
