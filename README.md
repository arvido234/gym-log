# Gym Log - Modern Fitness Tracker 

**Gym Log** is a sleek, privacy-focused Android fitness tracker designed to help you organize your weekly workout schedule and track your progress with ease. Built with a modern "Deep Charcoal" & "Electric Blue" dark mode aesthetic, it offers a clutter-free experience for serious gym-goers.

##  Key Features

*   **Weekly Schedule**: Organize your exercises by day (Monday - Sunday) to keep your routine structured.
*   **Quick Logging**: Log your sets, reps, weight, and RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) with a single tap.
*   **Progress Tracking**: Visualize your strength progression with interactive history charts for every exercise.
*   **Customizable Experience**: 
    *   Toggle input fields (e.g., hide RPE if you don't use it).
    *   Add subtitles/notes to exercises (e.g., "Leg Day").
    *   Fully localized in **top English** and **German**.
*   **Data Privacy**: All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud accounts, no tracking, no ads.

##  Design

The app features a polished **Material Design 3** interface with a high-contrast Dark Mode, ensuring readability in gym environments while saving battery life.

##  Data & Customization

*   **Move & Edit**: Easily move exercises between days or rename them as your routine evolves.
*   **Export/History**: View your complete training history at a glance.
